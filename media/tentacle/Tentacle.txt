* What is tentacle? *
The tentacle is my first player model for Quake2. It is based on the tentacles in 
Day Of The Tentacle. 


* Play Information *
UNZIP into \quake2\baseq2\players\tentacle
You should now have the following files in the directory:

tris.md2	the tentacle
weapon.md2	his gun model
tentacle_i.pcx	the multiplayer select icon
tentacle.pcx	skin
weapon.pcx  	gun skin
tentacle.txt	this file
