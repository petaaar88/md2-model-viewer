Maximillion - Quake 2 player model, by Conor O'Kane (conor@matrix.ie)

max.zip should contain the following files:

  • tris.md2
  • weapon.md2
  • weapon.pcx
  • max.pcx
  • max_i.pcx
  • max_red.pcx
  • max_red_i.pcx
  • max_blue.pcx
  • max_blue_i.pcx
If any of the above are missing, you can get the the complete .zip (which may be a newer version) at http://indigo.ie/~wip3out/quake2.html

You should unzip all these files into quake2\baseq2\players\maximillion.

There are no sounds with this player yet. I might dig out the old VHS and record a few beeps and whistles at some stage - if enough people use Max.

Maximillion is one of the robots from a film called "The Black Hole". He's the bad guy - Vincent's arch enemy (make sure to put him on the opposite team as Vincent in those Eraser Bot team games - watching those two fight is almost as good as the film ;-)

My next project is Bob from the same film. (This is turning into a Black Hole player pack!)

Tools used:

  • 3D Studio MAX 1.2
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Paint Shop Pro
  • Quake 2 Model Editor
  • NPherno's Skin Tool
