Vincent - Quake 2 player model, by Conor O'Kane (conor@matrix.ie)

vincnet.zip should contain the following files:

  • tris.md2
  • weaapon.md2
  • weapon.pcx
  • vincent.pcx
  • vincent_i.pcx
If any of the above are missing, you can get the the complete .zip at http://indigo.ie/~wip3out/quake2.html

You should unzip all these files into quake2\baseq2\players\vincent.

Vincent is one of the robots from a film called "The Black Hole". So far I've only made one skin for this model, but I may get round to making red and blue capture the flag team skins. Watch out for my next two Black Hole models - Bob and Maximillion.
