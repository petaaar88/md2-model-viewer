MODEL DESCRIPTION:
Darth Maul, Sith Lord, apprentice to Darth Sidious.
He battled Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi, resulting
in damage to his garments: now he wears a tunic for
deathmatch.

Other info              : See--light sabers really can penetrate walls.  

Additional Credits to   : Credits to Id Software; LucasArts.

Thanks to               : All Star Wars fans

* Play Information *

New Sounds              : Yes.  The sound pack is kind of a walking symphony 
                          to recreate the aura of the character.  Credits go 
                          to John Williams for the original score. 
CTF Skins               : Yes
VWEP Support            : Could be implemented with different colors for the 
                          light-saber, like the Darth Vader model.

* Construction *

Poly Count              : 832 polygons, plus 8 polys for the weapon.
Vert Count              : 420 vertices
Skin Count              : 4 skins
Base                    : completely new model
Editor used             : - QME to model & animate 
                          - NST & PSP to skin 

* How to use this model *

Unzip all files to the quake2/baseq2/players/Darth_Maul directory.
