               - Special Unit Warrior Plug & Play Model Sounds -
                 ------------------------------
           made by: Iron Saint.(luftkinder@msn.com)
               

This .zip will replace the sounds  for any Q2 model you choose with
sounds from the "Close Combat 2 CD".


Note: This will only work with Q2 version 3.12 or later.

Instructions: 
	Unzip these .wav files into your quake2\baseq2\players\[model] 
	directory.


all sounds are borrowed from the Close Combat 2 CD!  .
