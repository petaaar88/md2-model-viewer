[MODEL NAME]		Rapaza

[AUTHOR]                AreopagitA

[INSTALLATION]		Create a subdirectory called "Rapaza" on your 						"quake2\baseq2\players" and put the files on it.

[FILES INCLUDED]	rapaza.txt    This file
			tris.md2      The model
			weapon.md2    The weapon model
			rapaza.pcx    The model skin
			weapon.pcx    The weapon skin
			rapaza_i.pcx  The model icon for Q2

[UTILITIES USED]	Used Q2 Modeller to edit the sailor mercury model.
			Used NPherno's Skin Tool & Paint Shop for skin.
